I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at [Soongsil University](https://math.ssu.ac.kr/).

Prior to joining Soongsil University, I was a postdoctoral research fellow in the [Department of Bioengineering at Rice University](https://bioengineering.rice.edu/) under the mentorship of [Oleg Igoshin](https://igoshin.rice.edu/). 

I received my Ph.D. from the [Department of Mathematical Sciences at KAIST](https://mathsci.kaist.ac.kr/home/en/), where I conducted my research under the mentorship of [Jae Kyoung Kim](https://mathsci.kaist.ac.kr/~jaekkim/).

My research interests lie in analyzing biological systems using mathematical tools, with a focus on spatiotemporal dynamics, AI-driven data analysis, circadian rhythms, immunotherapy modeling, and single-cell RNA sequencing.

Although I am a mathematician by training, I have extensive experience collaborating with experimental biologists and doctors.
